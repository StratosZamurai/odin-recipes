Authentic Pho

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup consisting of bone broth, rice noodles, thinly-sliced meat (in this recipe, it's beef), and fresh garnishes.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds beef soup bones (shank and knee)
  • 1 medium onion, unpeeled and cut in half
  • 5 slices fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 2 pods star anise
  • 2 and a half tablespoons fish sauce
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1 (8 ounce) package dried rice noodles
  • 1 and a half pounds beef top sirloin, thinly sliced
  • Half cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon chopped green onion
  • 1 and a half cups bean sprouts
  • 1 bunch Thai basil
  • 1 medium lime, cut into 4 wedges
  • One-fourth cup hoisin sauce (Optional)
  • One-fourth cup chile-garlic sauce (Optional)
