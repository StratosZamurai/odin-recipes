Double Layer Pumpkin Cheesecake

Description

A thick, creamy cheesecake base topped with a layer of spiced pumpkin cheesecake filling sits on a graham cracker crust for this easy layered holiday dessert recipe.

Ingredients

Cheesecake Layer:

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • Half cup white sugar
  • Half teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Pumpkin Layer:

  • Half cup pumpkin puree
  • Half teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground cloves, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg, or more to taste
